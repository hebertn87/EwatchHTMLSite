Description


Super Smash Brothers Melee for the Gamecube has always been the biggest hit. It may be older than more recent titles, but melee boasts one of the highest fan and playerbases in all of esports. Melee has been the top dog of its fighting genre class, and continues to pull in many viewers every year. Players will face off in ladder style competition, to determine who is the best super smash player, in many hosts of tournaments that happen each year. Most matches play a series of games, and whoever wins the majority moves on to the next match, until a final winner is decided.
