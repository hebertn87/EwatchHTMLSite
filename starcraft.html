Starcraft is a Real Time Strategy game, or RTS, created by Blizzard Entertainment. The purpose of the game is to manage your troops, resources, and destroy the enemy. Players must be extremely skilled to manage everything, achieving rates of 200-500 actions per minute. This game is extremely popular in eastern countries, such as South Korea.
